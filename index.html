About Us

More Than Just a Golf Club

Koster Golf Club is a proud, community-driven sporting hub nestled in the heart of Koster, North-West Province. We are far more than a 9-hole golf course — we are a gathering place that brings the whole community together.

From our vibrant sports bar to our competitive Bowls team, from Saturday morning parkruns to Wednesday evening joker draws and pool nights — there is always something happening at Koster Golf Club for everyone.

We are committed to growing the sport at grassroots level, supporting youth development, and creating a welcoming environment for players, families, and visitors alike.

On-Course Transport

Golf Carts

Enjoy your round in comfort with one of our golf carts. Carts are available for hire and make getting around the course quick, easy, and enjoyable — especially on a hot North-West day.

⚠ Important — Book in Advance

Golf carts are in high demand, especially over weekends and public holidays. Please book your cart before arriving at the club to avoid disappointment. Walk-in availability cannot be guaranteed.

To book a cart, contact the club directly on 072 410 8960 (Azel) or 076 916 3960 (Hannetjie).

Complaints & Suggestions

We take every complaint and suggestion seriously. Your feedback goes directly to management and helps us improve Koster Golf Club for everyone.

🔒 Your Privacy

All complaints and suggestions are treated as strictly confidential. Your name and contact number are required so management can follow up — this information will never be shared with other members or staff.

⚠ Anonymous submissions not accepted

As per club policy, complaints without a name and contact number will not be reviewed by the manager.
