About Us

More Than Just a Golf Club

Koster Golf Club is a proud, community-driven sporting hub nestled in the heart of Koster, North-West Province. We are far more than an 18-hole golf course — we are a gathering place that brings the whole community together.

From our vibrant sports bar to our competitive rollball team, from Saturday morning parkruns to Wednesday evening joker draws and pool nights — there is always something happening at Koster Golf Club for everyone.

We are committed to growing the sport at grassroots level, supporting youth development, and creating a welcoming environment for players, families, and visitors alike.

🏌️
Koos Reed Owner & Club Manager

Who We Are

Koster Golf Club is a proud, community-driven golf club based in Koster, North-West Province, South Africa. Under the ownership of Koos Reed, we are committed to maintaining quality facilities, growing the sport at grassroots level, and serving local players, youth, families, and visitors alike. Our club promotes sport, discipline, tourism, and the social development of our community — providing a safe, welcoming recreational environment for all who come through our gates.

What Your Sponsorship Supports

⚙️  Operational Support Diesel for greens maintenance & cutting  |  Equipment repairs & servicing  |  Water & irrigation systems
🏗️  Infrastructure Development Fencing upgrades  |  Clubhouse improvements  |  Security infrastructure  |  Practice facilities
🌱  Community & Youth Development Junior golf programmes  |  Youth training clinics  |  Local tournaments & events  |  Job creation opportunities

What You Gain as a Sponsor

Recognised as a key supporter of local development. Strong CSR alignment. Media exposure during events. Positive brand association with sport and community pride.
🌐 Networking Access
Invitations to club events & corporate golf days. Direct exposure to local professionals, business owners & community leaders across the North-West region.
